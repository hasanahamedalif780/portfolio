About

I am an aspiring PhD researcher with a strong passion for applying Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to address critical challenges in Earth System Science and Climate Research. My work spans climate forecasting, flood risk modeling, drought early warning systems, and renewable energy prediction using advanced deep learning and ensemble methods.

Currently completing my B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering at Dhaanish Ahmed College of Engineering (Anna University), I have published 6 journal articles in Q1/Q2 Springer Nature journals, with 4 additional manuscripts under review. I have also presented 13 papers at IEEE conferences covering climate anomaly detection, spatiotemporal modeling, and AI for healthcare.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities where I can contribute to cutting-edge research in AI-driven climate science, geospatial intelligence, and sustainable infrastructure planning under expert mentorship.
